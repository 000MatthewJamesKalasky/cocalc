SageMathCloud charges for subscriptions using the following pricing. Listed prices are in US dollars.

When charging in local currency, SageMath, Inc. will convert the prices listed into local currency using the conversion rates published by leading financial institutions.

You may sign up for a subscription in the billing tab and see a summary of upgrades in the upgrades tab.

Shared Resources

You may create many completely separate SageMathCloud projects. The projects that run on the general and members only servers all share common disk space, CPU, and RAM. They start with the free quotas below, and can be upgraded up to the indicated bounds on the right.


When you purchase a subscription, you can upgrade the quotas on any projects you use up to the amounts given by your subscription. Multiple people can contribute to increase the quotas on the same project, and may also remove their contributions at any time. You may also subscribe more than once to increase the amount that you have available to contribute to your projects.

Dedicated Resources

You may also rent dedicated computers. Projects of your choice get full use of the disk, CPU and RAM of those computers, and these projects do not have to compete with other users for resources. We have not fully automated purchase of dedicated computers yet, so please contact us at help@sagemath.com if you need a dedicated computer.
